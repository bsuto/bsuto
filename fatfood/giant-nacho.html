Fat Food Giant Stuffed Nacho

January 25, 2013(click image to load gif)


What if all the good nacho toppings were inside the chip? Cut two tortillas to chip shapes, and put taco meat, onions, refried beans and queso dip on one. Cover it with the other, crimp them shut, and brush vegetable oil over the top. Bake in the oven and it’ll crisp like a chip. Then you can top with sour cream, salsa, guacamole, and more cheese.
