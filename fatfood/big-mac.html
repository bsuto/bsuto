Fat Food Big Mac Casserole

February 5, 2013(click image to load gif)


Admittedly, I found this recipe online. You cook ground beef, onions, and pickles and put them in a casserole dish. Then layer american cheese and croissant dough over it, and bake. Take it out and cover with shredded lettuce and thousand island dressing. Each bite tastes like a Big Mac!
